## Spawn & Scouting

Spawned near waterfall basin, western Dune Desert edge. Waterfall visible ahead on spawn - water source confirmed immediately. Biomass (trees, shrubs) visible at ~8 o'clock from spawn along waterway shore.

Initial survey of waterfall basin nodes was discouraging - all impure. Continued scouting along the shoreline node corridor and discovered the starter cluster further along the cliff base.

**Lesson:** In Dune Desert, don't commit HUB to the first nodes you see. The initial basin nodes were all impure; the real cluster was a short scout away.
